# Official Store Badges

Since image generation quota was exhausted, please download the official store badges:

## Google Play Badge

Download from: https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/badges/

1. Visit the link above
2. Click "Build my badge" or "Download badge"
3. Save the badge as `play-store-badge.png` in the `public` folder

## Apple App Store Badge

Download from: https://developer.apple.com/app-store/marketing/guidelines/#downloadOnAppstore

1.Visit the link above 2. Download the "Download on the App Store" badge 3. Save the badge as `app-store-badge.png` in the `public` folder
